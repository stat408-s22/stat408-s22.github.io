Course Calendar and Materials

Each week, Tuesdays will be primarily lecture and class discussion or short activities, and Thursdays will be lab days.

  • You should bring your laptop with you to every class period.

  • Weekly labs will typically be due the following Monday in GitHub by 5pm.
  • Weekly homework assignments will typically be due the following Tuesday in D2L by 5pm.
